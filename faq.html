Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to schedule an oil pick up?

Depending the amount of gallons that are disposed every week we will calculate a rough estimate on the schedule pick ups.

How will I receive my payment?

Payment can be paid out with cash, check, money order, paypal, or any online payment.

How often do I need to schedule a Grease Trap service?

City regulation require establishments to clean out their grease trap every 3 months.

What are the current rates per gallon(s)/pound(s)?

Price of used fry cooking oil can fluctuate depending on the market. Current rate per gallon is $2.

How much is Pressure Washing in my establishment?

Customers get a free complementary Pressure Wash when they switch over with us. Pressure Wash will be used (free of charge) in the case of oil spillage caused by a defect of the container. Any addition Pressure Wash will cost .25 cents per square foot.

Do I need to pay for a container?

No, we will provide a container free of charge with the acceptable sizing for the establisment.

How do I prevent spillage in the container?

It's impossible to have oil spillages, but a good way to minimize spillage is to let the hot oil cool down and then proceed to dump off. Also, slowly pour the oil into the container so it won't splash against the metal container.

What kind of liquids can I put in the container?

You can put any type of fry cooking oil into the container.

What type of oil should I be using for my establishment?

Many restaurants use the standard fry cooking oil, but it can also be vegatable oil, peanut oil, fatty oils, etc.
